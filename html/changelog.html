13 March 2021

maxymax13 updated:

  • Items in your inventory now have an outline when you hover over them, originally from BeeStation. This can be disabled in Game Preferences.

12 March 2021

ATHATH updated:

  • Rabbits summoned by stage magic will start with 0 loaded eggs remaining instead of 10. You can still feed them carrots to cause them to generate more loaded eggs, though.
  • The speed boost from wearing the Easter Bunny head and/or the Easter Bunny suit has been nerfed, but is still present.

Fikou updated:

  • ninjas can now hijack the shuttle

Ghilker, Anturk updated:

  • fix pipes overlays when on layer other than 3

Ghommie updated:

  • refactored the voice of god. Specifically, I made the commands into singleton datums. It's also more robust against edge cases and bad text matches.
  • The voice of god spell should now work for AIs.
  • the voice of god commands themselves should tolerate the lack of whitespaces now.
  • Animals are no loner n(e)igh-immune to stuttering.
  • Silicons and animals won't keep stuttering or slurring forever after being struck by something that made them like that in the first place (such as a Blue Space Artillery).
  • Fixed the manhattan project reagent having two pound signs in its color variable.

Kathy Ryals & Timberpoes updated:

  • Pens now alert the stabbed person again.
  • Refactored attack() code pens and sleepypens.

Kush1Push1 updated:

  • Blood splatters only make your shoes or feet bloody if the tile is bloody enough,
  • Light steps trait prevents clothing from being covered in blood if you step on a bloody tile with your shoes off.
  • Blood dries over time. Usually, 3 or 6 minutes. If a splatter is big enough, it can stay wet up to 15 minutes.

Kylerace updated:

  • holodeck templates no longer have two entries each in the Template - Place verb

MMMiracles updated:

  • Adds a brand new map - Tramstation
  • Three segmented branches inter-connected via a transit system
  • 2 z-levels, with the lower being interlaced with maintenance tunnels to traverse the entire station through various ladder shafts and tram tunnels.

Mothblocks updated:

  • You can no longer alt-click and ctrl-click while inside the cryo tube.

Randomguy523 updated:

  • Fixed a typo for muted pAIs.

Ryll/Shaps updated:

  • Dogs are no longer able to maul you from inside backpacks or lockers. Unless they're void corgis, those pups are still more than capable of doing so!

Thalpy updated:

  • adds a wiki render button for reagent entries that will automatically write a table entry for you.

Timberpoes updated:

  • Food that should drop trash should now always drop that trash, including plates dropping from prepared food, food that you have to pull out of shells and gatfruits.
  • Agent ID cards now show the same SecHUD icons that their forged trim otherwise would instead of always showing as blank.

Y0SH1_M4S73R updated:

  • Pulsing a cut open wire on airlocks will now open it. Since cutting the open wire prevents airlocks from being opened by physical contact, this allows you to make airlocks only openable using assemblies. Try connecting a voice analyzer to the cut open wire.

Zytolg updated:

  • We've untangled some pipework that lead to Kilostations scrubber network feeding into the Xenobiology Air Distribution Loop

esainane updated:

  • The Atmospherics Crystallizer will no longer do wacky things with an empty recipe when "Nothing" is selected.

nianjiilical updated:

  • Sentient Diseases can now talk to themselves, and hear what the salty-ass ghosts of their victims have to say about their experiences with Space Ebola.

prodirus updated:

  • Jumpsuits should now drop attachments such as medals or ribbons when they are destroyed instead of making the attachments disappear as well.

11 March 2021

Arkatos updated:

  • Removed extra brute healing from Quadruple Sec drink for security personnel.
  • Removed extra brute, burn, oxygen and toxin healing from Quintuple Sec drink for security personnel.

Ghommie updated:

  • There is a new (backup) centcom announcer in town.
  • Uranium, bananium and bluespace golems and ethereals now properly show visual attack effects on do_attack_animation().
  • simple animals can now have their own visual attack effects instead of just punch or smash. e.g. carps bite and hivebots claw.
  • the slash attack effect now has a sprite.

LemonInTheDark updated:

  • Things that break pipes will cause much less game lag now. Looking at you singularity

Qustinnus updated:

  • Fixes Pwrgame icon not appearing

Zytolg updated:

  • Icebox Pipework Decongestion

necromanceranne updated:

  • Prevents martial arts warping people that they can view into their killing hands with the power of their martial prowess. And a missing adjacency check.

nightred updated:

  • Meta: Camera tags, wiring, power, and plumbing inspection completed and brought up to code.

prodirus updated:

  • Prevents Tranquility from being synthesized. No more appearing in botany plants!

tralezab updated:

  • bane back breaker fixed
  • Chefs and bartenders earn achievements for doing a good job!
  • Centcom now grades service at the end of a round.

wesoda25 updated:

  • Ethereals can once again channel power into APCs. Right click an APC with an open hand off of combat mode to do so (and to drain, do it on combat mode).
